Orders & Delivery

Cape Town Deliveries

The Grocery Dealer has next-day delivery for customers in the Cape Town area.

Bulk orders may require more time.

For bulk orders please email us at thegrocerydealer@gmail.com

Orders will only be processed once full payment has been received, unless a prior agreement has been made.

Our courier flat rate for Cape Town is R60.

Cape Town Surrounds Deliveries

Delivery times for areas surrounding the Cape Town area has a 2-5 day delivery time.

Bulk orders may require more time.

For bulk orders please email us at thegrocerydealer@gmail.com

Orders will only be processed once full payment has been received, unless a prior agreement has been made.

Our Courier flat rate for areas outside of Cape Town is R100.

Collections

We also offer a collection service from our warehouse at 15a Hummingbird Avenue, Cape Town, Western Cape.

We offer same-day collections on orders placed before 10am and next-day collections on orders placed thereafter.
